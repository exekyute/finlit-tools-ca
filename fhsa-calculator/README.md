# FHSA Optimization Engine

> Projects a Canadian First Home Savings Account year by year — contributions, tax refunds, and growth — toward a down-payment target.

## What it does
- **Projected Down Payment** — total balance at withdrawal year.
- **Total Tax Refunds** — refund value generated by contributions.
- **Out-of-Pocket Cash** — how much you actually put in.
- **Portfolio Composition Bar** — your cash vs. reinvested refunds vs. market growth.
- **Year-by-Year Table** — opening balance, deposits, return, ending balance. CSV exportable.
- **Warning Banners** — fires when you hit the $40K lifetime cap or when the 15-year FHSA expiry rule kicks in.

## Why it's useful
- Toggle "Reinvest Tax Refunds" to see the snowball when refunds roll back into the next year's contribution.
- Honors the $8K annual base + $8K max carry-forward + $40K lifetime ceiling.
- Surfaces the 15-year account-life rule (transfer to RRSP / buy / withdraw cash).

## How to run it
Double-click `index.html`. Or use the hosted hub at the repo root.

## How to use it
1. Set your annual income (drives the simplified marginal tax rate).
2. Set your expected return rate.
3. Set your annual contribution and timeline.
4. Toggle refund reinvestment on or off.
5. Read the composition bar and year-by-year table. Export CSV if needed.

## Canadian rules it follows
| Rule | Value |
|---|---|
| Lifetime contribution cap | $40,000 |
| Annual base contribution room | $8,000 |
| Max carry-forward of unused room | $8,000 |
| Account life | 15 years (banner fires at year 15) |

## What it does NOT model
- Province-specific brackets or surtaxes (uses a simplified 5-band combined rate).
- The FHSA-to-RRSP tax-free transfer mechanics on expiry.
- Qualifying-home rules and the 30-day occupancy requirement.
- Excess-contribution penalties.
- HBP / FHSA combined use.

## Tech notes
One self-contained HTML file. React 18 via CDN, Tailwind via CDN, Inter font, Babel in-browser. Inputs persist in `localStorage`.

## Disclaimer
Illustrative only. Real FHSA room depends on when you actually opened and contributed. Verify your room and unused carry-forward in **CRA My Account** and consult a Certified Professional Accountant before acting. Not tax, legal, or investment advice.
